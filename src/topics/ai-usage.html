AI Usage

Discover how Artificial Intelligence (AI) was used in this project.

AI was an excellent learning partner and researcher on the Dweb stack. Copilots were used for programming tasks such as prototyping, refactoring, rewriting existing functions, debugging and scanning through complex messages and logs. I authored most of the content and have indicated where content was written by Generative AI.
