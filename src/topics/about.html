About

The orange heart project mixes art and Dweb technology through a heart-centered perspective to explore addressing and resilience. The gallery component presents stencil-based spray art on birchwood.

Some random thoughts

The decentralized web us a resilient system based on sharing digital assets all over the world, while still being able to prove that they are genuine.

That theme dovetails with stenciling. Stencils provide visual consistency that builds upon itself through repeated reuse.

Of course, stencils that are used over and over again degrade. Art degrades on any surface whether it is concrete or paper. Especially stencils, the more you reuse the stencil, the more paint it picks up and the image loses sharpness.

The stencil isn't just reused, its repurposed. The gallery show is a series of images created by a fairly old stencil, whereas the online component includes photographs of the world where the stencil acts as a frame. In both cases, their edges are softening. Those are some of my thoughts, you can click below to see my bio.
