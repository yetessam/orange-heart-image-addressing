Orange Heart Project

Orange Heart Project demonstrates dweb technology and explores addressing and belonging.

I first started stenciling hearts in my neighbourhood as part of a healing journey. Since 2017, they have been reused and repurposed on a variety of mediums beyond concrete.

Heart-framed images captured from May - August 2024.

Note:
This project repurposes a heart-shaped stencil created as a response to gun violence in San Francisco's Mission District.
