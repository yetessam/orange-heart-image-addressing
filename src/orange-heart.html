Orange Heart Emoticon Significance

Comfort, care, and serenity, love, admiration, and support may be represented by the orange heart symbol. Meaning is in the eye of the beholder, the Dutch love orange, it can denote support for the Indigenous Residential School survivors in Canada. Orange is both a noun and adjective and certain high visibility oranges are used for cautions and warnings.
